# 🛡️ Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition (CCDC)

This repository documents my participation in the Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition (CCDC), a hands-on cybersecurity competition focused on defending enterprise environments against real-world attacks.

---

## 🧠 Overview

The Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition (CCDC) is a nationally recognized cybersecurity event where teams act as defenders (blue team) responsible for securing an enterprise network while a professional red team actively attempts to compromise it.

Participants must balance security, system administration, and business operations under time pressure.

---

## 🎯 Responsibilities

- Monitored systems and network activity for signs of compromise  
- Responded to active attacks and security incidents in real time  
- Identified and mitigated vulnerabilities across Linux and Windows systems  
- Maintained uptime and availability of critical business services  
- Collaborated with team members in a fast-paced defensive environment  

---

## 🔧 Skills Applied

- Incident response  
- Log analysis and monitoring  
- System hardening (Linux & Windows)  
- Network defense and traffic analysis  
- Vulnerability remediation
